GeoReSeT experiment review

What the experiments show so far

We are testing whether French Wikipedia text and geolocated article coordinates can produce useful land-cover supervision. The core result: article text contains real signal, but label reliability, relevance, class imbalance, and image-model data coverage matter more than another compact text rewrite.

Research Arc

At A Glance

1. Text is informative

Aligned article text beats shuffled controls. For Qwen CORINE raw content, balanced accuracy is 0.281 versus 0.067 shuffled.

2. Ground truth quality matters

Only 646 of 1,251 articles pass point-label share >= 0.8 at 250 m. On that cleaner subset, Qwen raw-content balanced accuracy rises to 0.352.

3. Relevance is the best filter

Medium/high land-cover relevance plus 250 m spatial purity gives the strongest text diagnostic: Qwen raw-content balanced accuracy 0.409.

4. Compact text is lossy

Evidence cards, highlights, and retrieved windows preserve signal, but none clearly replaces the full raw article for the main benchmark.

5. Models trade off differently

Gemma improves raw CORINE accuracy and OSM exact/micro metrics, while Qwen is stronger on balanced CORINE behavior.

6. Image probing is promising

Frozen CLIP plus a linear probe trained on broad weak labels reaches 0.600 accuracy, far above zero-shot CLIP at 0.200.

Current Research Position

Best concise interpretation: Wikipedia text is useful for land-cover supervision when articles are relevant and point labels are spatially reliable. The best text input remains raw content. Deterministic summaries, cards, highlights, and retrieved windows are useful for diagnostics, filtering, and cost control, but not as primary replacements. For image learning, broader weak-label coverage beats the strictest text-agreement filter at the current data size.

Recommended Next Experiment

Train with broad weak labels

Keep the broad `all` or `spatial_only` training pools so rare CORINE classes remain represented.

Use quality as a soft signal

Use relevance, spatial purity, quality score, and model agreement as weights or sampling controls, not hard exclusions.

Move beyond generic CLIP

Test a Sentinel-native encoder or fine-tuned vision backbone against the frozen CLIP linear-probe baseline.
